Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI hairstyle generation work?

Upload a selfie and choose a hairstyle. Our AI generates a photorealistic image of you with the selected style, preserving your face perfectly.

Is Color Lab really free?

Yes! Color Lab uses on-device processing to preview hair colors instantly. No credits or subscription needed.

How do I restore my subscription?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases." Your subscription is tied to your Apple ID and will restore automatically.

Why doesn't my result look right?

For best results, use a front-facing photo with good lighting, a neutral background, and your face clearly visible. Avoid hats, sunglasses, or heavy shadows.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to iPhone Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > SwapHair Pro to manage or cancel.
